Additional Challenges

These are engaging exercises that are not needed for the flow of the course but for students who finished the first parts early.

Drawing Challenge: 'H'


Challenges:
• Add the letter 'H' to spell out 'AMNH'.
• Use the new commands penup(), pendown(), and backward(), to write the first letter of your first name (if it's one of 'A', 'M', or 'N', choose another letter).
