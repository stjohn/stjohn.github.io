Drawing AMNH


Challenge: On a piece of paper, draw out each line of 'A' and 'M'. Mark with a '^' when you lift your pen and a 'v' when you put it back down again.

Let's break up what you drew into steps:

  1. Lift up the pen and move a bit to the right, so that the letters are not connected.
  2. Draw the first vertical line.
  3. Turn the turtle to draw the diagonal line.
  4. Draw the diagonal line.
  5. Turn the turtle to draw final vertical line.
  6. Draw the vertical line.
