Fly Between Cities

In our previous program, we "stamped" the following locations:

  • Los Angeles: (-118,34)
  • Santo Domingo, DR: (-69, 18)
  • Tokyo, Japan: 140 degrees east, 35 degrees north
  • Auckland, New Zealand: 174E, 36S
  • A city that you would like to visit (latlong search tool).


Challenge: Change the earlier program to trace out the path between cities?

Hint: A small change with the pen will show the flights between the cities marked (under the Mercator projection).



Challenge: Reorganize your trip to minimize your flying time.
