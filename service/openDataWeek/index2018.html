Hunter College OpenData Showcase
7 March 2018

A Hands-on Workshop Exploring NYC OpenData

As part of NYC OpenData Week, come join this hands-on workshop exploring publicly available datasets using the Python programming language.

  • Wednesday, 7 March, 4:30-6pm
  • Hunter College, 68th & Lexington
    (Directions)
  • Register via EventBrite
  • Assumes no previous experience
  • Bring your own laptop
    or use one of ours.

We'll start with a showcase of what's possible with NYC OpenData and its uses across the computer science curriculum at Hunter College. Next, we will:

  • Getting started with Python: using turtles to map GIS data of subway stops,
  • Reading data from spreadsheets (using pandas): who gets the most parking tickets,
  • Combining pandas & turtles: mapping NYC libraries, and
  • Making HTML maps (using folium): mapping WIFI locations and 311 calls.
