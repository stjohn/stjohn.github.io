Exam Rules

  • The exam is closed book and closed notes.
  • When taking the exam, you may have with you
    • pens or pencils,
    • a two-sided 8.5" x 11" page of notes.
  • You may not use a computer or calculator.
  • All books and bags must be left at the front of the classroom during this exam. This is to keep the aisles clear, so that the instructor can easily reach people to answer questions.
  • You must be enrolled in the course to take the exam.

How to Prepare

  • Do the assigned reading.
  • Make sure you do all the problem sets and quizzes that have been assigned. The problems (or parts of them) make excellent exam questions.
  • Work through the sample and past exams. These (or variations of them) often appear on exams.
