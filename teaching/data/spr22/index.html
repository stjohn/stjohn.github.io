CSci 39542: Introduction to Data Science
Department of Computer Science
Hunter College, City University of New York
Spring 2022

Announcements:

  • December 8: We have a classroom (1036HN) for the spring semester and will be in-person! Thank you to President Raab for making this possible.
  • The course prerequisites for this programming-intensive class are:

    • a year of college-level programming in the same language (CSci 127 & 133) or (CSci 135 & 235)),
    • at least one semester in Python (i.e. CSci 127), and
    • a course in statistics (i.e. Stat 213 or equivalent).

    If you are interested in the course and have a year of programming, Python proficiency, and taken statistics, but they don't align with the Hunter prerequisites, you can enroll via CUNYFirst. If you took the courses elsewhere, reach out to a computer science advisors about what's needed for permissions.

  • Enrollment for the course is via CUNYFirst. Spring enrollment opens in mid-November.
