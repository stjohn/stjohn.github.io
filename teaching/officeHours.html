Office Hours

Fall 2024 office hours are by appointment.

To get assistance:

  • For information about internships, regular email announcements about opportunities are sent out automatically to all declared computer science majors.   • For other questions, the best way to reach me is via email: stjohn AT hunter.cuny.edu.
